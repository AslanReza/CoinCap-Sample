Welcome to CoinCap, your reliable source for cryptocurrency market data

CoinCap's goal is to give you the most real-time view of cryptocurrency trading taking place around the world. Built to meet the needs of cryptocurrency enthusiasts, CoinCap adapts to the unique demands of cryptocurrency markets.

Crypto Market Vs. Traditional Market

Cryptocurrency Market

Constant activity, no open or close of trading

Assets are sold across many exchanges

Digital assets can be purchased with a variety of methods, including fiat currency and cryptocurrency

Less standardization on how exchanges operate

Traditional Market

Trading floor opens and closes; not available on nights and weekends

Commodities only available to purchase from select exchanges

Purchasing options limited to fiat

Exchange operations are highly regulated with many standards

By aggregating exchange data from around the world, we provide fast calculations with controls to detect and remove outliers. This ensures that you're always seeing the prices that best represent the global market 'as-is.'
